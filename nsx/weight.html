For the Volks, I weighed following: 45.6 front wheel + tire and 54.0 rear wheel + tire. I could not find specs on these size S-03s, but similar size and performance Bridgestones weigh 24.2 front and 25.5 rear (with full tread). My tread was ~8/32" front and 7/32" rear or ~85% front and ~60% rear remaining. So, I guessed they weigh 24 front and 25 rear and used these figures to estimate the weight of the wheels.
